Big Data 4a Big Investment

 
Our project uses algorithms and big data to compare current loan applicants against previous loan applicants to provide you insight on whether lending money to that particular applicant would result in a successful transaction or a Default. For example, say that we have two people seeking a loan from Lending Club, Joe and Sandra;  Joe and Sandra are both current loan applicants and their stats are below:

  • Loan Length: 36 months
  • Location: California 
  • Reason: Vacation
  • Fico Score: 466
  • Annual Income: $45,000
  • Monthly Payment: $1,114

In our example above, it may not be a surprise that a general capacity analysis of the situation may show that Joe is would not be likely to fulfill a "Fully Paid" loan under those circumstances.

So lets look at Sandra's case; a general capacity analysis would show that she is most likely capable of achieving a "Fully Paid" status under those circumstances. But let us take a further dive into the analysis, and compare her specific loan stats to past loan applicants using KNN and a Vector Space model of data. Like the example shows there are certainly some things that can be analyzed with just normal eyes, however, other applicants may be a little harder to rule out, such as in Sandra's case. 

So the example shows a little glimpse into what we analyze, in actuality we analyze 15+ quantitative stats on each individual loan applicant. 

In our algorithms we use KNN to show the break down of the closest applicants that best match the applicant in questions, and we also use Vector Space Modeling to determine Danger areas, and help predict bad opportunities.
